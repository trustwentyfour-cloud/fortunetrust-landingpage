What We Do With Data

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26th October 2025
Organization: Fortunes Educational and Charitable Trust
Event: Breathe India 5kms Run

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal and payment information when you register for our marathon event through our website or payment gateway.

Information We Collect
When you register for the event, we may collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Contact Number
  • Age and Gender
  • Emergency Contact Details
  • Payment Details (processed securely via our payment gateway partner)
  • Health or fitness-related information (if applicable)

Use of Information
We use your information to:

  • Process your event registration and payment
  • Communicate updates related to the event
  • Provide emergency support during the event
  • Improve our services and future event experiences
  • Comply with any legal obligations

Payment Information
All online payments are securely processed by our trusted third-party payment gateway provider. We do not store your credit/debit card details on our servers.

Information Sharing
We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others. Your data may be shared only with:

  • Event partners (for coordination purposes)
  • Emergency services (only in case of a medical issue)
  • Government or legal authorities (if legally required)

Data Security
We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information using industry-standard security protocols.

Your Rights
You may request access to or correction of your personal data by contacting us at: careers@fortunetrust.org

Changes to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
